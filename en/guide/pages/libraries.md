# Your Library

The **Library** page is the heart of Playlite. This is where all your games live, whether they were imported from platforms like Steam or added manually.

## Managing Your Collection

You can filter, search, and organize your games.

### Adding a Game Manually

If the game is not from a platform (Steam, Epic, GOG, etc.), add it easily:

1. Click the **+ Add** button.
2. Type the game name.
3. Fill in optional information (Platform, Genre).
4. Click **Save**.

### Editing Information

Want to change the cover or fix the genre?

- Click the **More Options** button on the game cards.
- Select **Edit**.
- Adjust any field and save.

### Favorites

To highlight your favorite games:

- Inside the game details, click the **Heart** icon.
- The game will appear on the **Favorites** page.
