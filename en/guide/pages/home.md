# Home and Trending

On Home, you get a summary of your gaming life and suggestions on what to check out, as well as game suggestions based on your profile to try. In Trending, you find the games that are hot right now, making it easier to discover new releases.

## Overview

### Home

The screen is divided into smart blocks:

- **Library Summary:** Quick counters for how many games, favorites, and completed titles you have.
- **Continue Playing:** Quick access to the games you were already playing.
- **Recommended for You:** Games suggested based on your profile and history.
- **Players like you liked:** Popular games you may also enjoy.
- **Stats:** Shows your most-played titles and favorite genres.

### Trending

What you can do:

- See free games available right now
- Browse trending games
- See the most anticipated upcoming releases
- Open details and decide whether it makes sense to add them to your wishlist

## Tips

- Use **Wishlist** to save discoveries you do not want to add to your library yet.
- Combine it with **Libraries** to keep your collection organized.
