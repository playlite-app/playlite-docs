# Game Details

The **Details** page offers a deep view of each game in your library. Here you can find all the relevant information, from descriptions to gameplay statistics.

![Details](/images/app-details.png)

## What you'll find on the Details page

- **Cover and Title:** The game's cover image and official name.
- **Platform:** The platform from which you imported the game.
- **Description:** A summary of the plot or gameplay, with a button to translate it to pt-BR or view the original.
- **Gameplay Statistics:** Playtime and overall progress.
- **Details:**
  - Genre
  - Developer and Publisher
  - Release date
  - Series
  - Age rating
  - Estimated length
- **Quick Links:** Direct access to the official page, store, and communities.
- **Features:** A list of features such as multiplayer, theme, narrative, gameplay types, and more.
- **Steam Reviews card:** Other players' opinions about the title.
- **Adult Content card:** Indicates whether the game contains adult content.
