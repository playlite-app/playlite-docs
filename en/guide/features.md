# Features

Playlite is designed to be the home for your game collection, offering powerful organization tools without sacrificing privacy.

## Unified Library

Forget having to open multiple launchers just to remember what you have installed. Playlite centralizes everything in a smooth, fast interface.

![My Library](/images/app-library.png)

- **Local-first:** Your library loads instantly from your local database. No long loading times, no server dependency.
- **Search and Filters:** Find any game in milliseconds. Filter by status (playing, completed), platform, or genre.
- **Customization:** Covers, icons, and backgrounds are downloaded automatically, but you still have full freedom to edit them.

## Details and Tracking

Each game gets its own dedicated space. Keep a detailed record of your gaming journey.

![Game Details](/images/app-details.png)

- **Progress Status:** Organize your queue. Mark games as *Playing*, *Completed*, *Dropped*, or *Plan to Play*.
- **Personal Ratings:** Give your score and record how much time you have spent on the game.
- **Favorites:** Highlight the titles you love most for quick access.

## Steam Import

Bring your existing collection over with ease. Connect your Steam account to populate your library automatically.

- **Secure Sync:** We import your games and playtime via the Steam API. Your credentials (API Key and Steam ID) are saved **only locally** in your database, protected and never sent to third-party servers.
- **Updating:** Bought new games? Just run the import again to refresh your local library.

## Discovery and Planning

Not sure what to play next? Playlite helps you decide.

- **Trending:** See what is currently popular in gaming.
- **Wishlist:** Saw an interesting release? Save it to your Wishlist to check later.
- **Playlist:** Define your play order. A dedicated list for the next titles you want to try, removing choice paralysis.

### Smart Recommendations

Playlite suggests games based on two factors:

- **Your personal taste:** favorite games, ratings, and most-played genres
- **Community patterns:** players who liked the same games as you

Everything happens locally, with no data sent to the internet.
Recommendations are fast, private, and explainable.

## Backup and Security

Your data is yours. Unlike cloud services that can disappear, here you are in control.

- **Offline-first:** The app works 100% without internet (after the initial game data has been downloaded).
- **Portable Backup:** Export your entire library to a JSON file.
- **Restore:** Reformatted your PC? Just import the backup file and everything returns, including your ratings and statuses.
