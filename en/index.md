## Why use Playlite?

Unlike the heavy launchers from stores, Playlite focuses only on **organizing** your personal collection.

### Light and Fast

Built with modern technologies (**Rust** and **Tauri**), the app opens instantly and uses minimal system resources. Save
your RAM for your games.

### Immersive Visuals

Your library deserves to be seen. Playlite automatically downloads covers and metadata, offering a clean, modern, and
pleasant interface to browse.

### You Are in Control

* **Easy Backup:** Export your entire collection to JSON whenever you want.
* **No Tracking:** We do not collect usage data.
* **Open Source:** Transparent and auditable code.
